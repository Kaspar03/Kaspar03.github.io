About me

My name is Emanuele Gasparini, I'm currently an undergraduate student of Geology at the university of Padua, I hold a diploma in Informatics and Telecommunications obtained at ITTS Vito Volterra in the year 2022.

Since I was a little kid I had a big interest in Paleontology, Evolution and Science in general, compelling me to follow this academic path. Growing up I developed an interest for technology and informatics, leading me to learn various tools that I find now essential (e.g. Linux). In my free time I like to make naturalistic illustrations, read, hike, learn German and drink beer LOL.

I intend to keep this website entirely written in HTML, just for the fun of it, and most importantly for the retro vibes. Big Jurassic Park fan over here.
